# Research Notes

Research Notes contains preliminary philosophical observations, conceptual investigations, research questions, and developing arguments related to the **Interpretive Motivational Systems Framework (IMSF)**.

Unlike **Working Papers**, these notes are exploratory. They document ideas under development before they are expanded into full philosophical manuscripts.

---

## Current Research Notes

### 2. [Primary vs. Secondary Motivational Systems](research-notes/primary-vs-secondary.md)

### 3. [Meaning as a Primary Motivation](research-notes/meaning-as-primary.md)

Should meaning be regarded as an irreducible motivational system, or can it be explained through other motivational structures?

Explorations inspired by historical case studies including the Kolyma labour camps and other environments of extreme deprivation.
