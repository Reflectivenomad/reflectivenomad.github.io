# Reflective Nomad

*Independent Philosophy Research & Working Papers*

---

## Welcome

Reflective Nomad is an independent philosophy research workspace dedicated to developing, testing, and refining philosophical ideas.

Rather than presenting finished conclusions, this site serves as a **living archive** of working papers, research notes, and ongoing inquiry.

---

## Browse

- 📄 [**Working Papers**](working-papers.md) — Developed philosophical arguments in manuscript form
- ## Research Notes

The **Research Notes** document ongoing conceptual investigations related to the **Interpretive Motivational Systems Framework (IMSF)**.

Unlike the Working Papers, these notes are exploratory. They record developing arguments, conceptual distinctions, and open philosophical questions before they are expanded into full manuscripts.

### Current Research Notes

- 📘 **[Primary vs. Secondary Motivational Systems](https://reflectivenomad.github.io/research-notes/primary-vs-secondary/)**
  - What distinguishes a primary motivational system from a secondary modifier?

- 📘 **[Meaning as a Primary Motivation](https://reflectivenomad.github.io/research-notes/meaning-as-primary/)**
  - Is meaning an irreducible motivational system or a product of other psychological structures?

- 📘 **[Motivational Competition in Extreme Environments](https://reflectivenomad.github.io/research-notes/extreme-environments/)**
  - How do extreme conditions reveal the dynamics of motivational competition?

- 📘 **[Motivation and the Is–Ought Distinction](https://reflectivenomad.github.io/research-notes/is-ought/)**
  - Can a theory of motivation remain entirely descriptive without making normative claims?
